Combat System

AP (Action Points): Accumulates over time based on agility. Skills require a minimum AP to use.

Qi: Resource consumed by skills. Regenerates slowly during battle.

Cooldowns: Skills have cooldown periods after use.

Skills

断雨一击 (30 Qi, 60 AP) - 35 damage attack
回灯掌 (50 Qi, 40 AP) - Heal 40 HP
铁衣 (40 Qi, 50 AP) - +5 Defense buff
听雨势 (40 Qi, 50 AP) - Enables 伞骨八刺
伞骨八刺 (80 Qi, 100 AP) - 120 damage (requires 听雨势)

Tips

  • Click character action buttons in the room panel for quick interaction
  • Click exit names to move in that direction
  • Skills only work during combat
  • Use 听雨势 before 伞骨八刺 for maximum damage
