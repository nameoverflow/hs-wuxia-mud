Commands

go north or nMove north
look or lView current room
attack [id]Attack a character
talk [id]Talk to a character
perform [skill]Use a skill (in combat)
say [message]Say something

Combat System

AP (Action Points): Accumulates over time based on agility. Skills require a minimum AP to use.

Qi: Resource consumed by skills. Regenerates slowly during battle.

Cooldowns: Skills have cooldown periods after use.

Skills

Power Strike (30 Qi, 60 AP) - 35 damage attack
Healing Palm (50 Qi, 40 AP) - Heal 40 HP
Iron Body (40 Qi, 50 AP) - +5 Defense buff
Dragon Stance (40 Qi, 50 AP) - Enables Dragon Burst
Dragon Burst (80 Qi, 100 AP) - 120 damage (requires Dragon Stance)

Tips

  • Click on character names in the room panel for quick interaction
  • Click on exit names to move in that direction
  • Skills only work during combat
  • Use Dragon Stance before Dragon Burst for maximum damage
